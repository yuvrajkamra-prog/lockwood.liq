Welcome To Lockwood Distilleries

Renowned for its commitment to quality, Lockwood crafts exquisite whiskeys that are the epitome of smoothness, complexity, and character. Each bottle is a testament to time-honored distilling methods, enriched by carefully selected grains, pristine water sources, and expert aging in handpicked barrels. From the first sip to the lingering finish, Lockwood whiskeys captivate the senses with rich notes of oak, caramel, spice, and subtle smoke. Whether savored neat or on the rocks, every pour is a luxurious experience—refined, bold, and unmistakably Lockwood.

प्रवेश: COLLECTIONS

Pravash: Collections is the perfect introduction to the world of whiskey—crafted thoughtfully for beginners who are just starting their journey into fine spirits. Smooth, approachable, and mellow, Pravash: Collections offers a well-balanced flavor profile with gentle notes of honey, vanilla, and light oak.

प्रायः COLLECTIONS

प्रायः: Collections is not just a whiskey—it’s a legacy poured into a glass. Crafted for legends, this exceptional spirit is the embodiment of power, depth, and timeless sophistication. Every drop echoes years of mastery, aged in the finest oak barrels to deliver a bold yet smooth profile, rich with notes of dark chocolate, spiced oak, dried fruits, and a hint of smokiness. It’s a whiskey that doesn’t follow trends—it sets them. Reserved for those who lead with character and command respect
